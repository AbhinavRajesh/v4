An easy to use mood tracking app that takes your daily life's mood into account. It allows you to understand yourself better and helps you maintain an even mood throughout the day. The app has various features such as **graphs, statistics, insights, and relaxing sounds to improve you mood**.

I've written a blog explaining in detail about the tech-stack used, the motivation behind the project and the learnings. You can check it out <a href="https://blog.abhinavrajesh.com/introducing-aura-see-your-mood-in-a-whole-new-light" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">here</a>.
